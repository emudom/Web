About Us

Emudom (Pty) Ltd is a dynamic and innovative logistics, information technology and general supply company, with a strategic specialized task force, providing solutions throughout South Africa for a diverse range of customers. Our blend of skilled technicians, innovative professionals, strategic partnerships and guaranteed turnaround time coupled with excellent service, quick decision making and first-hand knowledge enables us to supply our customers with a tailor made, reliable and cost effective solution.

At Emudom, we aspire to grow our current scope through integrity, initiatives to supply advanced solutions to our customers and integration ability of elements within the supply chain and the various cultures involved across our region. This commitment remains the corner stone of our current operations and a vital part in our journey to grow.
